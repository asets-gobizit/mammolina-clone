What makes a Montessori education different from a traditional classroom setting? <\/div><\/span>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\n\t\t\t
\n\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

In a Montessori school, children aren't told what to do every minute. Instead, they choose activities that interest them. This makes learning feel like \"play\" rather than a chore. Because every child follows their own pace, it\u2019s a perfect fit for both children who need more time and those who want to move ahead quickly.<\/span><\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/details>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t
